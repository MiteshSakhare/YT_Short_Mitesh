# Part 7: The Weight of a Crown

NARRATOR:
Will you unleash the power thats been calling her name since birth?

She squeezed his arm once, hard, then released him. “Tonight. Tribunal. I’ll be in the gallery. If they try to take you…” Her hand dropped to her hip, where the dagger was hidden. “…they’ll have to go through me.”

She walked away, her stride confident, her tail flicking behind her.

Kaelen watched her go, the parchment in his pocket crinkling as his hand tightened into a fist.

Three factions, he thought. Four players. A prophecy. A tribunal. And a glutton demon who thinks my power tastes like home.

He laughed quietly, shaking his head.

This is what it feels like to have people who want you. Not your power. Not your bloodline. You.

He walked toward the dormitory, his steps slow, his mind already turning. The tribunal was tonight. Valerius would push for his detention. Seraphina would make a political play. Gaius would try to mediate. Vex’ahlia would watch.

And he would have to choose. Not who to be—that was already decided. But who to become.
