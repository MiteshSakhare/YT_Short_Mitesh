# Part 4: The Glutton’s Appetite

NARRATOR:
Is your power a curse, or an inheritance from the bloodline that binds you to darkness?

For a long moment, the two of them stared at each other across the candlelit table. Then Kaelen laughed—a genuine laugh, the first in this new life.

“You’re offering me a cage with golden bars and calling it freedom.”

“I’m offering you a throne,” she corrected. “With me beside it. The cage is your own making, Emperor. I’m just the one holding the key.”

He considered her proposal. The Demon Emperor in him raged against the leash. But the strategist—the part of him that had conquered three realms—saw the opportunity. She was powerful. Connected. And clearly, she wanted him not as a tool, but as a partner. That was a lever he could use.

“I have conditions,” he said.

Seraphina’s eyes gleamed. “Name them.”

“First, I play my own game. I’ll help you unite the demon houses, but I choose the methods. Second, the seal opens at my pace, not yours. I’ll take power when I’m ready, not when you decide. Third…” He glanced at Rin, who was watching him with undisguised fascination. “Your pet glutton stays away from my neck without permission.”

Rin pouted. “But you taste so good.”

“Denied,” Seraphina said flatly. “He’s mine. You can have scraps when I say so.”
