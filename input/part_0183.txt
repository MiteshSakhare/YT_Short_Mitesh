# Part 23: The Oracle’s Truth

NARRATOR:
When dawn breaks, ancient lines blur, and the forgotten awaken to reclaim their humanity.

Kaelen watched them, the darkness in his chest finally, fully, quiet. The silver ring on his finger pulsed with a light that was not his alone—it was Seraphina’s light, Rin’s light, Elara’s light, Vex’ahlia’s light. The light of everyone who had chosen to stand beside him, to believe in him, to love him.

He touched the ring, felt it warm against his skin, and smiled.

“Let’s go home,” he said.

End of Part 23
