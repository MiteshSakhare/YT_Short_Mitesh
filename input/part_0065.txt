# Part 9: The Bonds That Bind

NARRATOR:
What lies in her eyes, where shadows burn brighter than his own destiny?

He squeezed her hand. "One night."

Her grin was sunrise. "One night is a good start."

She pulled him down onto the bed, not with the force of a conqueror but with the ease of someone who had found something she'd been looking for. They lay side by side, her head on his chest, his arm around her shoulders, the moon painting silver lines across the chaos of her room.

The collar was heavy. The seal was waiting. Tomorrow, his past would arrive to judge him.

But tonight, there was warmth. Tonight, there was a girl who tasted his darkness and called it home.

Tonight, for the first time in three centuries, Kaelen Dragomir—once Demon Emperor, now Kaelen Vane—let himself be held.

End of Part 9
