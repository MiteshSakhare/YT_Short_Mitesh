# Part 1: The Fool’s Last Bow

NARRATOR:
Redbeards vessel shudders as Kaelen awakens, a ghostly echo of his own soul.

A world with magic. With noble houses that wielded it. And with… others. Creatures that lurked in the shadows, whose presence he could feel but whose true nature was still a mystery. A world far more complex than his old one.

“Apologies, Father,” Kaelen said, his voice a practiced, meek whisper. He pushed himself up, brushing dust from his fine tunic. “I was merely… contemplating the contract’s finer points.”

The Duke’s glare softened to a frustrated sigh. “Contemplating? You couldn’t even stay awake for the negotiation. You’re sixteen, my heir. You need to be sharper. The Academy will not be so forgiving. You leave in a week.”

The Academy. In his mind, it was a nest of vipers. The sons and daughters of the three great powers: the noble-human houses, the church of the Celestial Seers, and… the Demon Lords, who ruled from their dark continent but sent their children as a gesture of a fragile, hundred-year truce. A political powder keg disguised as a school.
